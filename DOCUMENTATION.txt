========================================================================
1.  INTRODUCTION
========================================================================
Easy Rigify aligns a Blender Rigify metarig to your character using
visible markers instead of manual bone editing. You place markers on the
mesh, the addon snaps the rig to them, generates the Rigify rig, and
gives you tools to bind and clean the skin weights.

Everything happens in the 3D Viewport sidebar under the "Easy Rigify"
tab. A status box at the top of the Markers tab always shows the
recommended next step.


========================================================================
2.  INSTALLATION
========================================================================
1. In Blender, open  Edit > Preferences > Add-ons.
2. Click "Install...", select the Easy Rigify .zip file, and enable it.
3. Make sure the built-in "Rigify" add-on is also enabled (search for
   "Rigify" in the same Add-ons list).
4. Open the 3D Viewport, press N to show the sidebar, and click the
   "Easy Rigify" tab.

The addon requires Blender 4.2 or newer.


========================================================================
3.  THE INTERFACE
========================================================================
The panel is split into four tabs along the top:

  MARKERS — Place markers on body, hands, and face.
  RIG     — Add the metarig, align it, generate the Rigify rig.
  SKIN    — Bind the mesh and clean the weights.
  TOOLS   — Fan bones and viewport utilities.

A "?" Help button (top-right) opens online documentation for the current
tab. Whenever a marker is selected, an on-screen help image and hint text
show you where that marker belongs.


========================================================================
4.  QUICK START  (the 5-minute rig)
========================================================================
1. Select your character mesh.
2. MARKERS tab > Body Markers > set "Body Mesh" to your model.
3. Click "Auto Detect Body". The body/leg markers appear.
4. Click "Place All Fingers" and place the fingers (2 clicks per finger),
   or use "AI Detect Fingers" if the AI runtime is installed.
5. (Optional) Open "Facial Markers" and run the face detect steps.
6. Click "Check All Markers" to confirm nothing key is missing.
7. RIG tab > "Human (No Face)" or "Human (With Face)" to add the metarig.
8. Click "Align Rig to Markers".
9. Click "Generate Rig".
10. SKIN tab > bind your mesh and clean the weights.

Done — you have a working Rigify rig aligned to your character.


========================================================================
5.  MARKERS TAB
========================================================================
The bottom of the tab shows how many body markers are placed. To check
your markers for problems (missing, overlapping, or sitting outside the
mesh), use "Check All Markers" — see section 5.6.

--- 5.1  PICK A BODY MESH ------------------------------------------------
Under "Body Markers", set "Body Mesh" to your character. This mesh is
remembered and reused by the detectors. IMPORTANT: apply scale and
rotation on the mesh first (Object > Apply > All Transforms) — unapplied
scale in particular causes bad alignment and binding.

--- 5.2  BODY MARKERS ----------------------------------------------------
"① Auto Detect Body" analyses the mesh and places the full body-and-leg
marker set: pelvis, spine, neck, head, shoulders, arms, elbows, hands,
thighs, shins, feet, toes, and heels. Marker sizes auto-scale to fit the
character. The scene is left as-is — no meshes are hidden.

If the AI runtime is installed, a "✦ AI Detect Body" button also appears
for neural placement on unusual proportions (see section 9).

--- 5.3  FINGER MARKERS --------------------------------------------------
Two ways to place fingers:

  • "② Place All Fingers" — a modal, two-clicks-per-finger workflow:
      first click = fingertip, second click = knuckle (base joint).
      The two middle joints are interpolated evenly between them.
      The right hand is mirrored automatically from the left.
  • The dropdown (arrow) next to it opens a per-finger picker so you can
    redo just one finger (thumb, index, middle, ring, pinky) or all.

"Straighten Finger Markers" projects the joints onto each finger's bend
plane to remove side-to-side wander while keeping the natural curl.

If the AI runtime is installed, "✦ AI Detect Fingers" places all finger
markers automatically (see section 9). Several sliders in the addon
preferences tune AI finger placement for thick/thin/stylised hands
(center radius, palm depth, knuckle radius, chain width tolerance,
straighten clamp).

--- 5.4  MIRROR & LIVE SYMMETRY -----------------------------------------
  • "Mirror L→R" copies all left-side markers onto the right.
  • "R→L" copies right to left.
  • "Live Symmetry" (toggle): while on, moving a left/right marker moves
    its mirror counterpart automatically, across the character's own
    symmetry plane. Turn it off for asymmetric characters.

--- 5.5  FACIAL MARKERS --------------------------------------------------
Open the "⑥ Facial Markers" section and toggle "Show Facial Markers".

  1. SELECT MESHES: point the addon at the relevant objects so detection
     is accurate:
       - Body Mesh (the face/head mesh)
       - Eyeball  — one mesh, or separate Left/Right eye meshes
       - Tongue
       - Teeth    — one mesh, or separate Top/Bottom
       - Eyebrow  — one mesh, or separate Left/Right brows
     Enable only the parts your character actually has.

  2. DETECT STEP BY STEP: run the detectors in order. Each places one
     region so you stay in control:
       Step 1 — Teeth & Tongue
       Step 2 — Brows & Eyes
       Step 3 — Lips
       Step 4 — Nose
       Step 5 — Eyelids
       Step 6 — Chin / Cheek / Jaw
       Step 7 — Forehead

Detected markers land on the mesh surface (eyelid and lip markers are
tucked a couple of millimetres inside so they follow the skin). Correct
any marker by hand, then use Mirror to copy a good side across.

--- 5.6  DIAGNOSTICS & DELETE -------------------------------------------
  • "Check All Markers" reports missing markers, markers sitting on top
    of each other, and markers that ended up outside the mesh — so you
    catch problems before aligning. Results appear in the Info bar.
  • "Delete All Markers" clears every marker from the scene.

--- 5.7  VIEWPORT HELPERS ------------------------------------------------
The Viewport box gives you:
  • Show / Hide Markers — hides the empties, their icons, AND their
    connection lines together.
  • X-Ray toggle — see markers through the mesh.
  • Mesh Selection toggle — lock the mesh so you only grab markers.
  • Marker Hints toggle — on-screen placement hints.
  • Marker Size slider (+ apply button) — resize all marker empties.


========================================================================
6.  RIG TAB
========================================================================

--- 6.1  ADD META RIG ----------------------------------------------------
  • "Human (No Face)"   — body-only Rigify metarig.
  • "Human (With Face)" — body + facial rig metarig.
Pick the one that matches whether you placed facial markers. (These are
also available from Add > Armature in the viewport.)

--- 6.2  ALIGN RIG TO MARKERS -------------------------------------------
"Align Rig to Markers" snaps every metarig bone onto your markers: pelvis
and spine chain, neck (split), head, shoulders, arms, hands, palms,
fingers, legs, and feet — including bone rolls derived from the mesh
surface for correct joint orientation. Run this after the metarig is
added and your markers are placed.

Note: aligning consumes the markers. Place and check them all first.

--- 6.3  GENERATE RIG ----------------------------------------------------
"Generate Rig" runs Rigify to build the final animation control rig from
the aligned metarig.

--- 6.4  REGENERATE & CUSTOM PRESERVE -----------------------------------
Lets you edit the metarig and regenerate the Rigify rig without losing
custom changes:
  • Set "Metarig" and "Generated Rig".
  • "Backup" stores your custom edits; "Restore" brings them back.
  • "Regenerate & Preserve" rebuilds the rig and re-applies the backup.

A "Report a Bug" button at the bottom opens the bug-report link.


========================================================================
7.  SKIN TAB
========================================================================
Binds your mesh to the generated rig and cleans up the deformation.

--- 7.1  BINDING ---------------------------------------------------------
Detect your meshes, then bind. "Smart Bind" runs an automated binding
pipeline; "Unbind" removes it. "Cleanup Weights" tidies the result.

--- 7.2  SURGICAL WEIGHT TIGHTENING -------------------------------------
When using the pipeline bind, a per-region "surgical" tightener sharpens
weights around specific areas:
    fingers · forearms · upper arms · shins · thighs · shoulders ·
    spine · neck
Each region has an on/off toggle and a "feather" amount. Presets
(DEFAULT / GENTLE / AGGRESSIVE) set sensible values. Spine and neck are
off by default in the presets — enable them manually if needed.

--- 7.3  WEIGHT CLEANUP & PAINTING --------------------------------------
A full weight toolkit:
  • Joint-bleed analysis and repair, weight smoothing (joints & twist).
  • Symmetrize weights across the mesh.
  • Prune tiny weights, remove unused vertex groups, select unweighted
    or over-influenced vertices, clamp per-bone influence.
  • Guided Enter/Exit weight-paint, deform-bone visibility toggle, and
    bone-region selection helpers.

8.  TOOLS TAB
========================================================================

--- 8.1  FAN BONES -------------------------------------------------------
Generate fan/helper bones for smoother deformation at the joints, and

--- 8.2  VISUALIZATION ---------------------------------------------------
Viewport and shading helpers for inspecting weights and deformation.


========================================================================
9.  OPTIONAL AI DETECTION
========================================================================
Easy Rigify includes optional on-device neural detectors for the body
and fingers. They are NOT required — the full geometric workflow works
without them.

INSTALL / UNINSTALL
  • Open  Edit > Preferences > Add-ons > Easy Rigify.
  • Click "Install AI Dependencies". This downloads a small on-device
    AI runtime.
  • RESTART BLENDER after installing so the runtime activates.
  • An "Uninstall" button removes it again if you don't want it.

Everything runs locally on your machine; nothing is uploaded.

WHAT IT UNLOCKS
  • "✦ AI Detect Body"    — neural body-marker placement (appears in the
                            Body Markers box once installed).
  • "✦ AI Detect Fingers" — neural finger-marker placement.

If the runtime isn't installed or hasn't been activated by a restart,
these buttons simply don't appear, and you use the standard geometric
detectors instead.


========================================================================
10.  TIPS & TROUBLESHOOTING
========================================================================
• APPLY TRANSFORMS FIRST. Unapplied scale or rotation on the body mesh is
  the #1 cause of bad alignment and binding. Object > Apply > All
  Transforms before detecting.

• CHECK BEFORE YOU ALIGN. Run "Check All Markers" first — aligning
  consumes the markers, so make sure they're all correct. It reports
  missing markers, overlapping markers, and markers left outside the
  mesh.

• MIRROR TO SAVE TIME. Place/refine one side, then Mirror it across. Use
  Live Symmetry for real-time symmetric adjustments.

• FINGERS: TIP THEN KNUCKLE. First click is the fingertip, second is the
  base knuckle. The middle joints are filled in automatically.

• FACE DETECTION NEEDS THE RIGHT MESHES. Assign the eyeball, tongue,
  teeth, and brow objects in "Select Meshes" for accurate results, and
  enable only the parts your character has.

• AI BUTTONS MISSING? Install the AI dependencies in preferences and
  restart Blender. If they still don't show, check the system console
  for install output.

• MARKERS HARD TO GRAB? Turn on X-Ray, enable Mesh Selection lock so you
  only select markers, and increase Marker Size.
