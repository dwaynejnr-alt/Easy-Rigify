Easy Rigify Documentation

Complete guide to auto-rigging characters in Blender

Welcome

Easy Rigify automates the tedious process of aligning a Rigify metarig to your character model. Instead of hand-editing dozens of bones in edit mode, you place visible markers on your character, and the addon snaps a production-ready rig into place.
